Projects I've Worked On

Get Outside

2022 - 2023

Get Outside is a 'disability-friendly location index'. This is my discretion project for my BSc Computing Systems course. The project aims to improve the confidence of people with disabilities. The data is community collected, and allows people with disabilities to create reports about locations so that others with similar disabilities can read these reports. Not only aimed at individuals, it also is aimed at carers to help them find places to take the people they care for out as well as businesses to promote their CSR as well as find areas that they can improve in.
